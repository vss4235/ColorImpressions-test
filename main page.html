Color Impressions

Color Impressions is a palette creation tool that offers insight into who you are. The tool is part of an ongoing research project aiming to explore people's personal relationships with color, as well as the influence of place on color perception.

By answering questions that explore your color preferences—rooted in memories, feelings, and personal experiences—you create a palette that offers insight into who you are and how you relate to color.

By collecting palettes from participants around the world, the project investigates how cultural, geographical, and individual experiences shape our connections to color.

The ColorCard Archive visualizes these shared and contrasting impressions, revealing patterns across people and places.
